5. Typography

Font familyNunito (Google Fonts — free)
"deal" + "insight"Nunito Bold (700), lowercase
"PIPELINE INTELLIGENCE"Nunito Regular (400), uppercase, letter-spacing 2.5px
"EVERY DEAL, IN SIGHT"Nunito Regular (400), uppercase, letter-spacing 2.5px
Word gap (deal → insight)~65px at 30px font size (proportional)
The gap between "deal" and "insight" should be slightly wider than a normal word space — enough to read as two distinct words, but not so wide they feel disconnected. The color change does most of the separation work.

6. Layout Rules

Bar width12px (at reference size)
Bar gap4px between bars
Bar corner radius3px (rounded top and bottom)
Bar heights (shortest→tallest)16, 26, 38, 50px
All bars alignBottom-aligned
Gap: bars → text~14px from right edge of tallest bar to start of text
Vertical boundaryAll text must stay within the top of tallest bar and bottom of shortest bar — nothing above or below
Do not:
• Place any text above the top of the tallest bar
• Place any text below the bottom of the shortest bar
• Add a dot/circle on top of any bar
• Use black text for "deal" or "insight"
• Use yellow/amber for text (only for bars)
• Change the bar order (must go amber → pink → purple → teal, short → tall)
